Legal and Practical

What you're
actually buying.

Lease assignment to July 2054. Assignment to third parties explicitly permitted.

You're acquiring the right to occupy and develop the land for the remaining lease term, approximately 28 years to July 2054. The entity selling is PT Paradise Path Ventures (director: Faith Lantz). The original lease (Akta Sewa Menyewa — notarised lease agreement) is between the landowners and Sergio Bertoni, who transferred to PT PPV in June 2023.

Pasal 11 (Article 11) of Akta No. 22 (June 2023) explicitly grants PT PPV the right to assign the lease to a third party. The buyer takes on the original lease terms. This is clean, notarised, and has legal basis.

Yes. Foreign nationals can hold leasehold land in Indonesia through a PT PMA (Penanaman Modal Asing — foreign-owned company). The lease assignment structure is designed for this. Your lawyer will want Akta No. 22, the PT PPV company documents, and the original Akta Sewa Menyewa No. 60 (notarised lease agreement, 2022). All are available on request.

Pondok Wisata (tourist accommodation) zoning. Permits villas, boutique resorts, guesthouses, and associated guest facilities. The zone requires tourism-use purpose; purely residential use is not permitted under this zoning.

Desa Suana, Kecamatan Nusa Penida, Kabupaten Klungkung. East coast of Nusa Penida. Approximately 45 minutes by road from the ferry terminal at Toya Pakeh, 45 minutes from Diamond Beach, 50 minutes from Kelingking Beach. Title: Certificate Hak Milik (freehold title) No. 02542/Desa Suana.

The lease assignment: 28 years, 2,346 m², Pondok Wisata zoning. The physical site preparation, including excavation, levelling, limestone perimeter wall, and road access, is part of the land as you find it. Architectural drawings from the previous project are available on request.
