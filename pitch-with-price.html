In the Water

Why people
come here.

Two ocean currents meet at Nusa Penida, producing the visibility, colour, and marine life that make this one of the most sought-after dive sites in Southeast Asia.

Manta rays swim in open water here year-round. Mola Mola (Ocean Sunfish) surface July to October. Rated SE Asia's top dive destination by Scuba Diving Magazine.

The land sits above this water. What you do with that proximity is yours to decide.

For Those Who Want More

The full picture

There are two main departure points from Bali, depending on where you are based.

From south Bali (Seminyak, Canggu, Uluwatu, Kuta): Sanur harbour is the main hub. Fast boats run to Toyapakeh on Nusa Penida throughout the day, roughly every 30–60 minutes. The crossing takes about 30–45 minutes. Toyapakeh is the closest port to Desa Suana.

From Ubud: Padangbai harbour is closer — around 1 hour by road from Ubud. Fast boats from Padangbai go to Buyuk and Sampalan on the north coast of Nusa Penida. Journey time is roughly 30–45 minutes. From Sampalan, Desa Suana is around 30–40 minutes by road.

There are no domestic flights to Nusa Penida. All access is by sea.

Desa Suana, Kecamatan Nusa Penida, Kabupaten Klungkung, Bali. East coast of Nusa Penida. Approximately 18 km from Toyapakeh port, around 40–50 minutes given road conditions. Diamond Beach and Atuh Beach are roughly 45 minutes away. Suana Point Beach is directly below the plot.

Pura Goa Giri Putri — the sacred Hindu cave temple — is in the same village. The cave entrance is less than 1 km from the plot boundary. It is one of the most visited spiritual sites on Nusa Penida.

Pura Goa Giri Putri · Suana Village · Photo: Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA)

The west coast is where day-trippers arrive. The east coast is where visitors stay: calmer, greener, less congested. The area around Desa Suana is anchored by Pura Goa Giri Putri cave temple and Suana Point Beach directly below. Infrastructure is arriving fast. Ferry from Sanur takes 30 minutes.

Pondok Wisata zoning permits villas and hospitality development: private villa compound, boutique resort, eco-lodge, or retreat centre. No concept restrictions.

Two ocean currents produce visibility and marine life divers travel the world for. Mola Mola surfaces July–October. Manta rays year-round. SE Asia's top dive destination. Source: Scuba Diving Magazine.

Lease assignment from PT Paradise Path Ventures to the buyer. Lease runs to 29 July 2054. Pasal 11 explicitly permits assignment. Transactions are cash. Indonesian banks do not typically finance foreign buyers.

This is a land-stage acquisition, not a finished asset. Utility connections (water and electricity) are not yet in place. These would be arranged as part of any development you undertake. If you do build, materials on Nusa Penida are shipped in from Bali, so construction costs run higher than on the mainland, so factor that into your budget for whatever you have in mind.

The lease runs to July 2054: 28 years from today. The boundary is secured, the access road is in, and the lease is ready to assign. What you do with the land, and when, is entirely your decision.
